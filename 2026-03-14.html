Social Media Influencers Amplify Toxicity and Power of Online Misinformation. A new study led by Cardiff Business School reveals that social media influencers generate significantly more toxicity than regular users when spreading misinformation online. Published in the journal Psychology and Marketing, the research analyzed brand-related misinformation spanning 47 brands across nine industries over a three-year period.
