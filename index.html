📚 文法重點 Grammar Highlights

從今天的文章中提取的 IELTS 重要語法結構

Technology

Participial Phrase as Adverbial Modifier

Pattern: Main clause + present participle phrase
The system is described as capable of emulating the functions of software companies, signaling Musk's ambition to expand beyond hardware.

A present participial phrase ('signaling Musk's ambition') is attached to the main clause to provide additional information about the result or implication of the main action. This is a sophisticated way to add detail without creating a separate sentence, highly valued in IELTS academic writing.

現在分詞短語（signaling Musk's ambition）附加在主句後，用於提供主要動作的結果或含義的附加信息。這是一種無需另起句子即可增加細節的精練手法，在雅思學術寫作中備受推崇。

Practice: The company launched its new product line, ___ (signal) a major shift in its business strategy and ___ (expand) into new markets.
Finance

Concessive Clause with Comparative Structure

Pattern: Despite + noun phrase, subject + continued to + verb
Despite this massive action, oil prices continued to climb on Wednesday, with Brent crude approaching $99 per barrel.

The 'Despite + noun phrase' construction creates a concessive meaning, showing that the result is unexpected given the circumstances. Combined with 'continued to + verb', it emphasizes persistence against expectations. This pattern is extremely useful in IELTS Task 2 for discussing contradictions.

'Despite + 名詞短語' 構成讓步含義，表明結果在該情況下出乎意料。結合 'continued to + 動詞'，強調違反預期的持續性。此句式在雅思 Task 2 中討論矛盾現象時極為實用。

Practice: Despite the government's ___ (intervene), unemployment ___ (continue) to rise, with youth joblessness ___ (approach) record levels.
STEM

Complex Noun Phrase with Post-modification

Pattern: Noun + past participle phrase + prepositional phrase
Researchers have achieved a groundbreaking milestone by successfully reviving neural activity in mouse brains that had been deeply frozen.

This sentence layers multiple post-modifiers: a relative clause ('that had been deeply frozen') modifying 'mouse brains', within a prepositional phrase ('in mouse brains') modifying 'neural activity'. This dense noun phrase construction is characteristic of academic writing and demonstrates advanced grammatical range.

此句疊加多重後置修飾語：關係從句（that had been deeply frozen）修飾 mouse brains，介詞短語（in mouse brains）修飾 neural activity。這種密集的名詞短語構造是學術寫作的特徵，展示了高級語法能力。

Practice: Scientists have made a significant ___ (break) by identifying genetic markers in patients who had been ___ (diagnose) with early-onset dementia.
International

Absolute Phrase with While-clause for Contrasting Actions

Pattern: Subject + verb + object, while + subject + verb + complement
European allies including France, Greece, and the UK have increased military support, while China urged both sides to exercise restraint.

The 'while' clause creates a sharp contrast between two simultaneous actions by different actors. This structure is particularly effective in IELTS writing for presenting balanced arguments or contrasting international responses to an event.

'while' 從句在不同行動者的兩個同時行動之間形成鮮明對比。這種結構在雅思寫作中呈現平衡論點或對比國際社會對同一事件的不同反應時特別有效。

Practice: Western nations ___ (impose) economic sanctions on the regime, while neighbouring countries ___ (advocate) for diplomatic dialogue and peaceful resolution.

✍️ 寫作素材 Writing Material

可用於 IELTS 寫作的好句子、搭配詞組和論點

Technology

Useful Phrases

  • signaling an ambition to (表明……的雄心) — The announcement signals Musk's ambition to expand beyond hardware and AI models into enterprise software.
  • the landscape is rapidly shifting (格局正在迅速變化) — The tech landscape is rapidly shifting as major players race to vertically integrate AI across their operations.

Collocations

  • roll out + product — Meta simultaneously rolls out its own in-house AI chips to support data center expansion.
  • race to + verb — Major players race to vertically integrate AI across hardware, software, and cloud infrastructure.
Model Sentence:
The announcement comes as Meta simultaneously rolls out its own in-house AI chips, the latest generation of its MTIA series, to support its massive data center expansion.

這一消息發佈之際，Meta 同時推出了其自研 AI 晶片的最新一代 MTIA 系列，以支持其大規模資料中心擴張。

💡 Writing Tip: Use 'as' clauses to show simultaneity between events. This technique allows you to pack more context into a single sentence, demonstrating the kind of complex sentence structure that IELTS examiners reward with higher band scores.
Finance

Useful Phrases

  • the largest ... in history (歷史上最大的……) — The IEA announced the largest emergency intervention in the agency's history to stabilize oil markets.
  • the combination of ... pushed (……的綜合作用推動了) — The combination of rising energy costs, geopolitical uncertainty, and a strengthening dollar pushed the Dow lower.

Collocations

  • climb + toward — Oil prices continued to climb toward $99 per barrel despite the emergency intervention.
  • disrupt + operations — Attacks near the Strait of Hormuz further disrupted maritime operations.
Model Sentence:
The combination of rising energy costs, geopolitical uncertainty, and a strengthening U.S. dollar pushed the Dow Jones Industrial Average to its lowest level since December.

能源成本上升、地緣政治不確定性和美元走強的綜合作用，將道瓊斯工業平均指數推至去年十二月以來的最低水平。

💡 Writing Tip: The 'combination of X, Y, and Z' structure is powerful for enumerating multiple causes leading to a single effect. In IELTS Task 2, this helps you demonstrate analytical thinking by linking multiple factors to outcomes.
STEM

Useful Phrases

  • achieve a groundbreaking milestone (取得突破性的里程碑) — Researchers have achieved a groundbreaking milestone by successfully reviving neural activity in frozen brains.
  • far-reaching implications for (對……有深遠的影響) — The findings could have far-reaching implications for organ preservation and space travel.

Collocations

  • restore + signaling — Targeted chemical treatments helped restore synaptic signaling after thawing.
  • represent + step toward — The research represents a significant step toward understanding how tissue survives extreme cold.
Model Sentence:
While full 'cryosleep' remains in the realm of science fiction, the research represents a significant step toward understanding how biological tissue can survive extreme cold.

雖然完全的「冷凍休眠」仍屬科幻範疇，但這項研究代表了理解生物組織如何在極端低溫下存活的重要一步。

💡 Writing Tip: The 'While X remains ..., Y represents ...' structure elegantly balances limitations against achievements. Use this in IELTS to show nuanced thinking — acknowledging what hasn't been achieved while highlighting what has.
International

Useful Phrases

  • failed to calm markets (未能安撫市場) — The IEA's record release of oil reserves failed to calm markets as crude continued to climb.
  • urged both sides to exercise restraint (敦促雙方保持克制) — China urged both sides to exercise restraint amid the escalating conflict.

Collocations

  • conflict + intensify — The US-Iran conflict intensified as three vessels were attacked near the Strait of Hormuz.
  • increase + support — European allies have increased military support in the region.
Model Sentence:
Meanwhile, the IEA's record release of 400 million barrels of oil from reserves failed to calm markets, as Brent crude continued to climb toward $99 per barrel.

與此同時，國際能源署創紀錄地從儲備中釋放4億桶石油，卻未能安撫市場，布倫特原油繼續攀升至接近每桶99美元。

💡 Writing Tip: Use 'Meanwhile' at the start of a sentence to introduce a contrasting or simultaneous development. In IELTS essays, this helps you structure parallel arguments and show how different aspects of a situation unfold at the same time.
